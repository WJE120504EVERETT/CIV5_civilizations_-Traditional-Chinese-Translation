In order to install the Mod properly, so the Leader Music will be played you must do the following:

1. In Muisca's Mod Folder, search for the MuiscaMusic.txt and MuiscaMusicDefines.txt and open them.
2. In your Civilization Install Directory search for the following:
"(Install Directory)\assets\DLC\Expansion2\Sounds\XML"
3. Open Audio2DScriptsExpansion2.xml and AudioDefinesExpansion1.xml from that folder
4. On Audio2DScriptsExpansion2.xml, paste the contents of MuiscaMusic.txt under <Script2DSounds>
5. On AudioDefinesExpansion2.xml, paste the contents of MuiscaMusicDefines.txt under <SoundDatas>
6. Save both files and enjoy your new civ with its own leader music.
